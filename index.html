Trim Drive recordings, right in the browser

Pick a video from Google Drive, cut the start and end, add metadata, and save it back to Drive. Everything runs locally in your browser — no server, nothing uploaded anywhere else.

The app can only see files you explicitly pick and files it creates (drive.file scope), never your whole Drive.
